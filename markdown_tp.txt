Devices

Explore our specialized marketplace for IoT devices, designed to meet the diverse needs of any business challenge. From tracking valuable assets to monitoring environmental parameters, our products facilitate real-time data collection and proactive management across various sectors such as Smart Farming, Smart Energy, Smart Buildings, Logistics & Transportation, Smart Health and more.

At ThingPark Market, we offer the world’s largest LoRaWAN catalog.

Our IoT Devices carrying the ThingPark Connected label are certified to ensure seamless integration with our ThingPark platforms, including ThingPark Enterprise and ThingPark Community (free cloud-based ThingPark Enterprise for limited non-commercial use). Additionally, we provide a variety of devices compatible with LoRaWAN, NB-IoT, and LTE-M protocols, catering to a broad array of communication needs and project scopes.

**Tips for choosing the right IoT Device:**

When selecting the perfect IoT device for your project, consider these refined tips:

- **Purpose and Use Case:** Define the specific functionalities required by your IoT device. Determine what data it must collect and the type of responses it should trigger.


- **Installation Environment:** Identify whether the device will be used indoors or outdoors. This influences the necessity for weather-resistant features or protective IP casing due to environmental conditions like humidity or temperature extremes.


- **Geographical Considerations:** Ensure the device complies with ISM band requirements for the specific location of your project, crucial for maintaining compliance and functionality.


- **Connectivity Needs:** Match your project requirements with the appropriate network connectivity. Whether it’s NB-IoT and LTE-M for real-time and heavy data communication needs (MBs of dataper device per day), the so-called Critical IoT, or LoRaWAN for any other use case, which falls under Massive IoT, selecting the right protocol is essential.


- **Future Scalability:** Plan for potential future growth by selecting devices that can integrate smoothly with expanding technological demands and scale with your project’s development.


These considerations will help you select an IoT device that not only meets your current requirements but is also adaptable to future expansions, ensuring long-term viability and compliance for your smart solutions.
